Who We Are

The Flying Nachos

We're a rec league hockey team built on one simple principle: play hard, have fun, and never say no to nachos. The Flying Nachos bring the energy of competitive hockey with the spirit of a team that knows how to enjoy the game.

Whether you're a seasoned skater or just finding your ice legs, there's a spot on this squad for you. We skate, we score, we snack.
